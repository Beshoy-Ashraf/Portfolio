About Me

The person behind the code

I'm a .NET Backend Developer with a deep obsession for clean architecture, meaningful commit messages, and systems that hold up under real-world load. My approach is grounded in SOLID principles — not because someone told me to, but because I've seen what happens when you don't.

I specialize in building production-ready RESTful APIs using ASP.NET Core and Entity Framework Core, with a sharp focus on security, scalability, and maintainability. From designing JWT authentication flows to architecting modular layered systems, I take ownership of the full backend stack.

My background in team leadership at Vodafone and Orange means I communicate clearly, handle pressure well, and ship reliably. I'm fluent in Arabic and English, allowing me to collaborate across diverse teams and clients.
